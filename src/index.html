Vibro Ripper

Manufactured by Almaahad Engineering for the first time in the Middle East.


MVR Vibro Ripper is an excavator attachment developed for high performance rock excavation and concrete demolition work.

HIGH PRODUCTIVITY

MVR Vibro Ripper has been designed to increase productivity and efficiency in 80% of applications up to 5 times more than Hydraulic Breakers.

ADVANTAGES & FEATURES

  • Low noise output.
  • Minimum maintenance.
  • Easy replacement tooth.
  • Economical and safety work.
  • No daily lubrication required.
  • Marine/underwater operation.
  • Higher productivity than Hydraulic Breaker.
  • More precision for separation of rock layers.
  • Powerful digging force by special link Structure.
  • With additional option devices, the ripper can be used as compactor or pile-driving hammer.
